Remarks

Single-node tree crossover chooses a node position in two trees and swaps those nodes, including their children. For example, if two TreeEntityBase objects represented as "(A OR B) AND C" and "B OR C" were to be crossed over at the "OR" node in the first tree and the "C" node in the second tree, the resulting offspring would be "C AND C" and "B OR (A OR B)".
